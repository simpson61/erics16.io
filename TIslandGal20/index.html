High Park in February

These pictures took in February of 2017. Usually during that month, it can get very cold. Sometimes, there's a lot of snow in the group. However, this year, the weather has significantly changed.

Due to climate changes, perhaps, almost all the snow has melted. So, I would like to go there and take pictures.

High Park is one of the the biggest park in Toronto. It contains small zoo, small trail, and closeby the subway station. If you walk toward the lake, there's a large beach beyond. During the spring month, there's Cherry Blossom trees. In that there, there's a lot of people go there and take Cherry Blossom.
