Toronto Test Market


Soon or later, Christmas season is coming! It happened every years around the Month of November to December.This annual events bring most of the traditional and charm of the European Market inspired old and new. (2)
Here is some more description about the Christmas Market.

Youtube video:

It happen around the Distrillery District, it is located in east portion of Downtown Toronto. It has some niche cafes which I like, and shop surround by heritage building. The distrillery is located mix of both commerical and residental area.
Here is some description of Distrillery District and its location.

From Travel Advisor (3):
The Distillery District is an internationally acclaimed village of brick-lined streets and dozens of vibrantly closed to traffic and just a few minutes walk from downtown Toronto.
