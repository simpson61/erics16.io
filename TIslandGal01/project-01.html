Centreville Amusement Park

These pictures are taken in Centreville Amusement Park. Centreville Amusement Park is the park located inside Centre Island. This park is really packed during the sumer. They don't operate in winter season. Centreville Amusement Park is essentally smaller version of Canada's Wonderland and Toronto Zoo.
