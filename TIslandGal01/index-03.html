Centre Island

These pictures are taken in Centre Island. According to Toronto.com, Algonquin Island is located between Centre and Ward Island. Centre Island is nestled between Ward's Island and Hanlan's Point. Visitors can rent bikes by the hour (standard, tandem and quad cycles) to ride the many bike trails throughout the island. During the summer months, you can rent a canoe or kayak.The beaches on the island's south side are supervised in July and August. There is a clothing-optional beach on the western Hanlan's Point.
