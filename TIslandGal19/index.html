Toronto's 183th birthday at City Hall

It's 2017. This event was organized because it is Toronto's 183th birthday. But at same time it is Canada's 150th anniversity. Yes, it is the big significant celebration. For the references those who did not have chance to see this events, there will be more festivals related to 150th celebration as the season continues.

Back to 183rd birthday, this event held it for two days (March 5th to 6th). on sunday there was market from 30 local vendors, it inlcudes the Food trucks. On Monday, there was DJ skating, of courses there's Food trucks and some shopping vendors. There was also hot chocolates.
