Evergreen Brickwork

Evergreen Brickwork, known as Don Valley Brickwork is the former industrial places that is convert into city park.The Brickwork is another Toronto's well known landmarks along with Casa Loma and City Hall.When the factories closed down, it was convert into environmetnally friendly places. They redesign land to have ponds, tullip trees, and mud creek. They also offer environment friendly products to the visitors. In addition, they offer interactive workshops and community festivals.

When I took these pictures in January 22nd, almost all the snow has been melted. In addition, there was Holiday Festival occurred where they have a lot of food trucks and drinks to offer. Usually, they hold Farmer Market in there as well.
